## What we are building (one paragraph)

A **multi-tenant Progressive Web App (PWA)** for **dealers and distributors** in **Nepal**: sales bills (print/PDF), stock, purchases, customer payments and credit, returns, expenses, company overview — **Supabase** (Postgres + Auth + RLS) with **RPCs** for money and inventory. Currency **NPR**; Bikram Sambat in places; payment modes Cash / Credit / UPI-style labels. Each dealer is a **tenant** (signup → approval → isolated data).

---

## Who uses it

- **Primary:** Distributor / dealer owner or accountant (warehouse / depot).
- **Geography:** Nepal first.
- **Vertical:** FMCG, cold-chain, groceries, beverages, hardware, general wholesale.
- **Language:** English UI; optional Nepali marketing line later.
